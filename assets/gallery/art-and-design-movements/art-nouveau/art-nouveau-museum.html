Permanent Collection

The organic beauty of fin de siècle

Art Nouveau brought a new understanding of form, line, and material at the turn of the twentieth century. Our collections trace that vision in full depth — from stained glass and jewellery to typography and architectural ornament.

Year-round — Galleries A1–A4

Light through Glass — Stained Glass of the European Art Nouveau

An extensive collection of stained glass from the workshops of Tiffany, Gallé, and their Central European contemporaries. Each panel is installed so that natural light passes through it across the course of the day, continuously transforming the character of the space.

New acquisition

An Art Nouveau salon from a Viennese villa — an interior intact

After two years of conservation work, we present a fully preserved salon interior from 1903, relocated from a private Viennese villa. Furniture, wallcoverings, light fittings, and decorative objects form a rare, coherent whole.
