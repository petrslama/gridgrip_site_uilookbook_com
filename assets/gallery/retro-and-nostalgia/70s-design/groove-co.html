Our Story

Born From Passion,
Built on Sound

We opened our first shop in New Orleans' Marigny neighbourhood in 1971, stocking shelves from a single suitcase of imports. Today we run five stores across Louisiana — but the obsession hasn't changed one bit.

Every record is hand-graded, cleaned and priced by people who actually listen. Come in for a browse and stay for an hour.
