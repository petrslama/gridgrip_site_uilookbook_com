The Studio

Craft as a patient practice

"The pot is the potter — unhurried, attentive, honest."

We do not teach ceramics as a hobby. We teach it as a discipline that rewards sustained attention and tolerates no shortcuts. Clay remembers every hesitation and every conviction.

Our studio is calm, unhurried, and equipped for serious work. Three electric wheels, two kick wheels, a slab roller, and kilns fired in earthenware, stoneware, and raku.

View Schedule
  • Small cohorts Never more than 12 students per session. Every student receives individual attention at the wheel.
  • Materials included Stoneware and earthenware clay, underglazes, studio glazes, and kiln firings are all covered in course fees.
  • Open studio access Students enrolled in ongoing courses may use the studio independently during off-peak hours.
  • Czech & English taught All courses are available in both languages. We also welcome Erasmus and international students.
