The House

A Century of
Deliberate Excess

Founded in Paris in 1923, Maison Élysée has always occupied the space between restraint and opulence. Our perfumers do not blend — they compose, the way a conductor shapes a symphony from individual instruments.

Noir Élysée is the house's most ambitious statement: a fragrance built for those who move through rooms and are remembered long after they've left.

  • Natural ingredients sourced from heritage suppliers in Grasse, Assam, and Yemen
  • Aged in Baccarat crystal vessels for a minimum of six months before release
  • Each bottle individually numbered and signed by the head perfumer
