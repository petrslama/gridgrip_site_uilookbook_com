The House Offers

Crafted for Lingering

Every amenity chosen not for abundance, but for harmony with the house's character and its unhurried pace.

Garden Restaurant

Seasonal menus composed around the rhythms of the Ardennes larder. Breakfast is unhurried; dinner is an event.

Cellar Bar

Natural wines, Belgian lambic, and small-batch spirits in a vaulted space unchanged since 1908.

Spa & Hammam

A discreet retreat on the lower ground floor. Botanical treatments, steam room, and private soaking tubs.

Reading Library

Curated shelves of design history, literature, and illustrated tomes. Open to guests around the clock.

Cycle Hire

Restored Gazelle city bikes for exploring the neighbourhood, the parks, and the canal paths beyond.

Morning Ritual

Hand-poured single-origin coffee and fresh viennoiseries delivered to your room — no dining room required.

Getting Here

Ixelles, Brussels

Set in the quietly prosperous streets of Ixelles — ten minutes from the Grand-Place, five from the Horta Museum, and a short walk from the finest Nouveau buildings in private hands.

  • Brussels-Central — 12 min by tram
  • Brussels Airport — 35 min by train
  • Horta Museum — 7 min on foot
  • Palais Royal — 15 min on foot
